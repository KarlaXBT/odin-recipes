Lasagna Recipe

Recipe for Delicious Homemade Lasagna

This is a classic recipe for heart warming homemade lasagna straight from my Italian grandmothers hand written recipe book.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680g) tomato sauce
  • 6 oz (170g) tomato paste
  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil or 1 tsp dried Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until softened.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil (or Italian herbs), salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles over the sauce, then spread some ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  9. Add a layer of meat sauce, followed by a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella.
  10. Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, mozzarella) until ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and remaining Parmesan on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  13. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to set.
