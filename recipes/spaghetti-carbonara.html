Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Classic Italian Pasta with Creamy Egg Sauce

A simple and authentic spaghetti carbonara recipe featuring eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti
  • 4 oz (115g) pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Salt, for pasta water

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat a skillet over medium heat and cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic in the last minute, then remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated cheese.
  4. Drain spaghetti, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  5. Quickly toss hot spaghetti with the egg-cheese mixture, stirring vigorously to create a creamy sauce without scrambling eggs.
  6. Add pancetta and garlic to the pasta, tossing to combine.
  7. If sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water a little at a time to loosen it.
  8. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
