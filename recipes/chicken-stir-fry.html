Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

Quick and Healthy Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

A fast and flavorful chicken stir-fry loaded with fresh vegetables and a savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless skinless chicken breast, sliced thin
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (slurry)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken slices, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken and set aside.
  3. Add garlic and ginger to the pan and saut√© for 30 seconds.
  4. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and snap peas; stir-fry until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. Return chicken to the pan.
  6. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil.
  7. Add cornstarch slurry and cook for another 1-2 minutes until sauce thickens.
  8. Serve hot over cooked rice.
